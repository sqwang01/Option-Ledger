Google Sheet sync

Paste the Web App URL from your Apps Script deployment. Your positions are read from and written to that sheet.

One-time setup
1. Open a new Google Sheet → Extensions ▸ Apps Script.
2. Paste the Code.gs script I gave you, then Deploy ▸ New deployment ▸ Web app.
3. Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone.
4. Copy the /exec URL and paste it above.

Best option: paste that URL once into the SHEET_WEBAPP_URL constant near the top of this file's code, and it connects automatically every launch — no localStorage needed.
